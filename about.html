Why We Started
Wateright

Wateright Plumbing was founded right here in Riverside, CA with a simple mission — provide fast, honest, affordable drain cleaning and plumbing service to our community without the big company runaround.

With years of hands-on plumbing experience, we saw firsthand how frustrating it was for homeowners and property managers to deal with overpriced contractors who don't show up on time or explain what they're doing.

So we built something different. A local operation where you talk directly to the person doing the work. Where the price you hear is the price you pay. Where we show up when we say we will and leave your home cleaner than we found it.

We're not a franchise. We don't have a call center. When you call Wateright, you get Jason — and Jason gets the job done.
